About Me

I'm a music lover, weightlifter, and research enthusiast. I study the effects of nuero-modulation on human subjects. What does this mean? I'm interested in how various neuro-technologies effect a person's mental and physical state and applying those insights to solve real world problems.

My ultimate goal is to use research from health care innovation and neuroscience to help treat stress, anxiety, and neurodegenerative diseases. I love solving problems and finding new ways to increase efficacy in research. I've designed, built, executeed, and conducted qualitative and quantitaive studies from start to finish.

When I'm not working you can find me biking around Boston, at the gym, or eating thai food. I love taking online classes and attending Meetup events.
